WONDER HALL 5.0 QUICK START

1. Open Wonder Hall and choose Museum Studio.
2. Create or enter the local Studio password.
3. Recreate galleries and resources.
4. Use Preview Draft to inspect the museum without publishing.
5. Open Publish and connect GitHub.
6. Click Publish Draft to GitHub.
7. Refresh phones and tablets after GitHub Pages updates.

Your draft and published website are deliberately separate.
